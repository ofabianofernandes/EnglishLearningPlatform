Reading Comprehension Exercises

Improve your reading skills with these exercises:

Read the Passage and Answer the Questions

Once upon a time, in a small village nestled in the mountains, there lived a kind old man. Every day, he would tend to his garden, planting seeds and watering the plants. The villagers often saw him speaking to his plants, and they wondered if the plants grew better because of his kind words.

Questions:

  • 1. What did the old man do every day?
  • 2. How did the villagers feel about the old man's habit of talking to his plants?
  • 3. Where was the village located?

True or False

Indicate whether the following statements are true or false:

  • 1. The old man lived in a large city. (True/False)
  • 2. The villagers thought the old man's habit was strange. (True/False)
  • 3. The plants seemed to grow better when the old man spoke to them. (True/False)

Summary

Summarize the following passage in 2-3 sentences:

The sun was setting behind the hills, casting a warm glow over the landscape. The children in the village were still playing, their laughter echoing through the streets. It was a moment of pure joy and peace, a reminder of the simple pleasures of life.
